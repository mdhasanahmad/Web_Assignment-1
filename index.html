Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating new ideas, products, or solutions that improve existing systems and drive progress. It involves thinking creatively to address challenges and transform possibilities into reality.

Sustainability

Sustainability refers to meeting present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own. It involves balancing economic, environmental, and social factors to create long-term value while minimizing harm to the planet.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the fundamental physical and organizational structures needed for the operation of a society, such as transportation systems, energy supply, water management, and communication networks. It supports economic activities and enhances the quality of life.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of granting individuals or communities the authority, confidence, and resources to make decisions and take control of their own lives. It fosters self-reliance, independence, and the ability to achieve personal and collective goals.

The Vision of Future

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Digital Transformation

Integrating technology into society to enhance connectivity and access to services,invention new things, amke life easier, improving quality of life and innovation.

Green Energy

Green energy harnesses renewable resources like solar, wind, and hydropower, reducing emissions and promoting environmental sustainability worldwide.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure integrates advanced technology, sustainable materials, and efficient designs to support urbanization, connectivity, and economic growth.

This transformative approach encourages businesses, governments, and individuals to prioritize social impact and sustainability over profit maximization"
